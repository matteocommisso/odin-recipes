Sanji's Fruit Salad

Description

Have you ever watched One Piece and been like... "Dayum. That dish Sanji made looks skrimdidilidumptious. I wish I could make that at home!" Well wish no longer for I will now teach you how to make Sanji's Famous Fruit Salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 Orange
  • ¼ of a Pineapple (Approx 200g)
  • 6 Strawberries
  • 1 Banana
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of Lemon Juice
  • 30ml of Liqueur (Kirsch etc.)
  • 2 Tablespoons of Granulated Sugar
  • Mint leaves for Garnishing

Steps

  1. Peel the orange (including the white fibers if you’re being fancy) and remove the sections with a carving knife one by one. Do this over a bowl*** and set aside any juice that’s left over.
  2. Peel and core the pineapple, and then cut into bite-sized pieces. Remove the stems and halve the strawberries.
  3. Put the fruit from step one into a bowl, and add a mixture of the leftover orange juice, lemon juice, liqueur and sugar.
  4. Leave the bowl to cool for 1-2 hours in the refrigerator.
  5. Just prior to eating, cut the banana into coins of about 1cm thickness, mix with the chilled bowl of fruit and serve in a fancy bowl. Garnish with the mint leaves.
