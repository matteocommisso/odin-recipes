Grilled Cheese Recipe

Description

Have you ever thought to yourself, "Wow, I really love grilled cheese. I wish someone could teach me how to make this delicious, complex, prestigious dish." Well look no further, because I got you covered.

WARNING. This dish is one that only the most masterful of chefs could create (if they are lucky). Get ready becasue the secrets of the legendary grilled cheese are about to be unveiled.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  2. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese
  3. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese
  4. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  5. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
